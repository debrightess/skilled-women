About Us

Investing In Women, Transforming Nigeria

Every woman deserves the chance to discover her strength, develop her skills, and build a life of dignity and independence.

Bertha Onyeneke, Co-Founder & Trustee

The Strength of a Skilled Woman Foundation is a registered Nigerian nonprofit dedicated to empowering women through vocational training, entrepreneurship support, and access to education. Incorporated in January 2026, we believe that when a woman is skilled, her family, her community, and her nation rise with her.

Stories of Change From Our Beneficiaries

Before I joined the Foundation's tailoring programme, I had no income of my own. Today, I run my own fashion label from home and can pay my children's school fees. This foundation changed my life completely.

Amaka Okafor
Beneficiary & Tailor, Port Harcourt

The mentorship and business training I received gave me the courage to start my catering business. I now supply food to three offices in my area and employ two other women. I am proud and grateful.

Ngozi Ihejirika
Beneficiary & Caterer, Rivers State

I was a school dropout and thought my future was limited. The Foundation's literacy and ICT training opened doors I never knew existed. I am now a certified digital assistant and have financial independence.

Blessing Tamuno
Beneficiary & Digital Assistant, Rivers State
