Your Donation Changes A Woman's Life

Every naira you give goes directly towards training, mentoring, and supporting women across Nigeria. There are no middlemen — just real impact, real women, real change.

₦5,000

Covers learning materials and tools for one woman entering a vocational training programme.

₦15,000

Sponsors a woman through a full month of digital skills training, including ICT and e-commerce basics.

₦30,000

Fully funds a woman's completion of a 6-week certified vocational skills programme from start to graduation.

₦100,000

Provides a startup micro-grant to help a skilled woman launch her own small business and become financially independent.
