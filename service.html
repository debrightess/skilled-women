What We Do

How We Empower Women Across Nigeria

Our programmes are designed to address the real barriers that prevent women from reaching their potential — from lack of skills and capital, to limited access to education and healthcare.

Vocational Skills Training

We run structured, hands-on training programmes in tailoring, catering, cosmetology, soap and candle making, and more. Each course is designed to give women a certified, job-ready skill within weeks — not years. Participants graduate with the knowledge and confidence to earn income immediately.

Our Causes

Entrepreneurship & Business Development

A skill alone is not enough — every woman deserves the tools to turn her talent into a sustainable business. We provide training in business planning, financial management, pricing, record-keeping, and customer service. We also connect participants to micro-grant opportunities and markets to sell their products and services.

Our Causes

Digital Literacy & ICT Training

The digital economy is full of opportunity — and we make sure women can access it. Our ICT programme covers computer basics, internet navigation, social media for business, e-commerce, and mobile money. We equip women to market their skills online, reach more customers, and manage their finances digitally.

Our Causes

Education & Literacy Support

Literacy is the gateway to every other opportunity. We sponsor adult literacy classes for women who missed formal education, and provide scholarship support for girls at risk of dropping out. We believe no woman should be held back by a lack of access to learning — no matter her age or background.

Our Causes

Health & Wellbeing Programmes

A woman cannot build a future if her health is neglected. We run community health education workshops covering reproductive health, maternal wellness, mental health awareness, and nutrition. We also link women to healthcare providers and support services, ensuring they can access care when they need it most.

Our Causes

Advocacy & Gender Rights

Systemic change requires a voice. We engage community leaders, local government, and policymakers to champion equal opportunities for women in education, employment, and public life. Through awareness campaigns, community dialogues, and grassroots mobilisation, we challenge discrimination and build a culture where every woman is valued.
